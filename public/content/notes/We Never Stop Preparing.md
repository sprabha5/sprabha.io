Dear Diary!
It's 7 March 2026, 2 PM.

I think everyone who is preparing for something should read these books: *Tension Mat Le Yaar*, *Jana Zaruri Hai Kya*, *Masala Chai*, *Sarkai Chai Ke Liye*, *Yaar Papa*, etc. They will be able to relate to the content of these books. As a refresher, instead of scrolling on the phone, they can give their time to something like this.

I am not preparing for something like competitive exams anymore. I am already done with that phase, and I am out of it now. But I agree with the fact that we never stop preparing for something — we are always in that phase. While juggling between jobs, we again enter into that phase.

I don't know if this applies to people who never switch jobs or leave a company for something else, but for those who do, this is definitely applicable.

I have already completed some of these books and have many more to complete. If you have suggestions for other good books, please let me know — I would be happy to read them. Also, if you would like me to give feedback or a short review of any of these books, let me know.

Thanks.
